Founder

Comfort Titilayo Orija-Adesoye founded the over 50’s West african women’s forum in the UK in August of 2012 (registered Charity).

The motivation to start the group came as a result of membership with AWAD (Association of Women Art Dealers) International, a charity umbrella organisation of African women.

The purpose of the group is for West African women over 50 and retired women who want to advocate for themselves, mentor their young ones, share their wisdom, experience, and celebrate their culture the opportunity to be able to do so.The group is one of the network of AWAD International and is proud to be one, as AWAD International has helped identify and develop various skills and organisation of the group.
