Oranyan Heritage Foundation (OHF)

Mrs Comfort Titilayo Orija-Adesoye - Founder OHF
The Alaafin of Oyo - Lamidi Adeyemi III - Grand Patreon OHF

The OHF has responsibilities in ensuring developmental initiatives in Oyo, South-west Nigeria. Such works range from data and information preservation to the exploration of all the traditional, economical and historical heritage of the legendary Oranyan and Oyo at different perspectives relating to their fundamental developments.

You can find out more by checking our About page.
