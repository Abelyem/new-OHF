About Us

The Oranyan Heritage Foundation (OHF) is a not-for-profit and non-governmental organization, set up with 21st century developmental challenges in focus. It's founded to lift the socio-cultural and economic value of the host community, Oyo and its environs. Within the OHF focus, the Museum of Black Women In History and other projects of OHF are also not-for-profit.

Oranyan Heritage Foundation (OHF) is registered in Nigeria as a not-for-profit and non-governmental organization. OHF is founded to lift the socio-cultural and economic value of the host community, Oyo and its environs. The OHF has been charged with so many responsibilities in ensuring developmental revolutionaries in Oyo societies ranging from data and information preservation to the exploration of all the traditional, economical and historical heritage of Oranyan and Oyo at different perspectives relating to their fundamental developments. Founded by Chief Mrs Comfort Titilola Orija-Adesoye, OHF is planned to be built on 50 acres of land donated by HRH, Alaafin of Oyo, Lamidi Adeyemi III.

The Alaafin of Oyo, Lamidi Adeyemi III, being the current custodian of the legendary Oranmiyan Omoluabi Odede, Great Prince of Ife, and the prime heir of Oduduwa, is the natural Grand Patron of Oranyan Heritage Foundation (OHF).

The OHF's MoBWIH took off with a foundation laying ceremony on the 13th January, 2021. The OHF's MoBWIH project took off as part of events that commemorated the 50th Coronation anniversary of Alaafin of Oyo, Oba Lamidi Adeyemi III who is also the Grand Patron of OHF.

Art and Culture Gallery / MoBWIH

This defines our ways of life and historical heritage which we shouldn't allow to go into extinction as a result of civilization agenda going on around the world. Preservation of our cultural beliefs and artifact is paramount to the establishment of Oranyan Heritage Foundation in ensuring that the people remain connected with their roots and respective generation.

Arts and crafts Exhibition centre

This centre would be a medium for arts and talents development. Establishing a vocational training centre is paramount to human capital development in every developed society, and Oyo Alaafin should not be an exemption. Youths and talented individuals would be nurtured and mentored under OHF initiative whereby helping to build the human capital of this region.

Development of OHF Museum

Showcasing of our collective art works as a people clearly shows our respect for culture and tradition of every walk of our life. Therefore, an ultra modern museum shall be setup within the OHF industrial estate to enhance our tourism and hospitality business. The museum would have a duo arrangement of physical and virtual/digital exhibition of art works from all walks of life.

Agriculture

Agriculture is the science and art ofcultivating plants and livestock. Agriculture was the key development in the rise of sedentary human civilization, whereby farming of domesticated species created food surpluses that enabled people to live in cities. It is practically the wealth of every nation when adequately propagated and managed.

Livestock Farming

This is another lucrative section of agriculture because meat consumption is practically inevitable among old and young. Our meat production channels would be from Fishes, Chicken, Cow, Goats and Pigs to support the meat supply chain in Oyo state. We are to put in place a well positioned cattle ranch, modernised fish ponds and other livestock animals HABITAT in such away that no other part of OHF industrial estate would be polluted or deprived of convenience as a result of the setup.

Processing of Agricultural products

Final consumers depends only on the processed agricultural products for their daily survival means. Therefore, OHF will setup a very sophisticated food processing section whereby common man food are sufficiently produced on a day by day production rate. Food products such as Garri, palm oil and meat production shall be hygienically made available at all times.

Food Production Farming

The farming section of the OHF industrial estate would focus largely on maize, cassava and yam plantation because of their diversified food processing capabilities. We shall allocate an acre each for these agricultural inputs in order to help the food supply chain within and around Oyo metropolis.

Hospitality Home Apartments

It is a global known fact that shelter is as important as food and clothing. For OHF, providing affordable housing scheme for the people of Oyo is important to their existence and sustainability for effective reproduction of all kinds.

Maternity and Childcare centre

The founder of OHF is a grandmother and mother whose passion for the well being of mother and child cannot be underestimated. We are proposing to build a state of the art Childcare and Maternity Center right in the middle of our OHF industrial estate is a perfect way to show that we are truly committed to Love.

Residential Apartments

OHF Residential Apartments is targeted at the young couples and newly employed unmarried singles. We intend to introduce a flexible payment package plan for interested individuals and we are proposing 5 blocks of detached duplex units.

Hostel Model Apartments

As part of OHF humanitarian impacted projects, we have thought of graduating students, postgraduates, NYSC members, fresh graduates and responsible singles concerning their access to short period accommodation services between 6months minimum to 24months rent at maximum as this would constantly gives room for regular maintenance and general revaluation of the facility. A 3 block of 32 rooms in each will give our hostel apartment 96 occupants every year.

Warehousing Facilities

Preservation of food stuffs, crops and valuable items needed to be adequately addressed by providing a reliable and conducive warehouse for their storage and from where it could be transported and distributed to final consumers or reproduction lines as the case may be. This is an all year round lucrative facility accessible to individuals, private and public sectors.

Integrated Commercial Hygienic Food Bank (For lease &community use)

This aspect of the entire project scope is vital to human existence. Therefore, locating this facility in our industrial estate is not a fluke because everybody relies on good food for their general survival. It is aimed to be sectionalized into 3 categories such as the OHF own food bank, public-own food bank, and private-leased food bank.

Commodity Storehouse

This is a facility for all interested entity for lease or hiring on temporary and long term basis depending on the kind of products to be warehoused such Cashew nuts, Cocoa, Shea butter nuts etc prior to processing or exportation .

Transit Safety Warehouse

This is extremely short term rentage warehouse for just transit purposes, and should be situated near to the expressway and occupants would not be allowed for more than 48hours per stay, otherwise could be seen as an emergency warehouse business practice.
